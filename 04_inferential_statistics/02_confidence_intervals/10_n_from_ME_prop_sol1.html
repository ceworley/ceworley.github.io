Do some algebra. \[ME = \frac{2 \sqrt{\hat{p}(1-\hat{p})}}{\sqrt{n}} \] Multiply both sides by \(\sqrt{n}\). \[ME\sqrt{n} = 2\sqrt{\hat{p}(1-\hat{p})} \] Divide both sides by \(ME\). \[\sqrt{n} = \frac{2\sqrt{\hat{p}(1-\hat{p})}}{ME} \] Square both sides. \[n = \left(\frac{2\sqrt{\hat{p}(1-\hat{p})}}{ME}\right)^2\] Simplify. \[n = \frac{4\hat{p}(1-\hat{p})}{ME^2}\]

Plug in numbers. \[n = \frac{4(0.27)(0.73)}{0.0029^2}\] Evaluate. \[n = 93746\]

The tolerance is \(\pm 1000\), so you can round to 2 significant digits, giving \(n=94000\).
